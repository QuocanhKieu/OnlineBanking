This Black History Month, we are excited to present the webinar "Echoes of the Empire: How History Shapes Modern Scotland." This collaborative event features Historic Environment Scotland, Dr Stephen Mullen, Dr Andrew Mackillop and researcher Cat Dunn from Glasgow University, and the Coalition for Racial Equality and Rights (CRER), focusing on the Empire report, a project by HES. We will delve into the project's initiation, processes, conducted research, and potential impact on all of us.

This report, linked below, sheds light on the past and offers an opportunity to see how history has shaped modern Scotland. The event will feature an in-depth panel discussion, followed by a Q&A session, we will be using Slido for audience questions.

Join us as we explore how the report can help us better understand our past and provide new perspectives that can positively impact our current communities.

Link to report:

Surveying and Analysing Connections between Properties in Care and the British Empire, c.1600–1997 (historicenvironment.scot)
